Class: Dog

Attributes:
breed (string)
hypoallergenic (boolean)
name (string)
weight (integer)
furColor (string)

Methods:
changeName (changes name attribute of dog)
getsFed (increases weight attribute by 1)
causesAllergicReaction (changes hypoallergenic attribute to False)
exercises (decreases weight attribute by 1)


Class: Plant

Attributes:
owner (string)
height (integer)
genus (string)
thorns (boolean)
numberOfFlowers (integer)

Methods:
prunePlant (decreases numberOfFlowers attribute by 3)
cutThorns (changes thorns attribute to False)
waterPlant (increases height attribute by 1)
sellPlant (changes owner attribute)
